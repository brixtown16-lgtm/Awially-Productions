# Video Assets

Add your video files here. The landing page expects:

- `drone-hero.mp4` - Hero background video (recommended: 16:9 aspect ratio, 30-60 seconds)
- `drone-sample.mp4` - Drone cinematography preview on landing page
- `brand-storytelling.mp4` - Brand storytelling video preview
- `product-launch.mp4` - Product launch video preview

## Video Requirements
- Format: MP4 (H.264 codec recommended)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9
- Duration: 15-60 seconds for previews, up to 2 minutes for hero
- File Size: Optimize to under 10MB for web (use tools like HandBrake or FFmpeg)

## How to Add Videos
1. Copy your video files to this folder
2. Name them according to the filenames above
3. Refresh your website

The videos will automatically play on your landing page and hero section.
